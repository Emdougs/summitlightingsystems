FAQ

Answers Up Front

Does it look noticeable during the day?

Chromaglow Pro is designed to be discrete on the roofline, with tracks/channels intended to keep the install looking clean and built-in. We color-match and place the system to minimize visibility.

Can you do warm white only?

Yes. Chromaglow Pro is RGBW, including a dedicated white channel. We can set warm-white-only daily schedules, with full color available anytime.

How do controls and scheduling work?

Control through the Chromaglow Pro app: presets, scenes, brightness, scheduled automation. Smart-home friendly with Alexa and Google Home.

How long does installation take?

Many homes are completed in a day; larger or complex roofs can take longer. We confirm timelines after measuring.

What happens if a section fails?

Sections can be cut and spliced for straightforward repairs. We isolate and replace using the manufacturer’s components.

Is it safe / low voltage?

Yes. It’s a 48V low-voltage system; local permit requirements vary by municipality.

Can I expand coverage later?

Usually yes. Controllers can manage long runs; we can add supplies/controllers as needed.

How do you handle steep roofs and hard access?

Certified in fall protection and MEWP operation; we plan installs with the right access method for your roofline.

What’s included in the quote?

Measured lighting plan, full cost estimate, timeline estimate, and warranty clarity for manufacturer parts + SUMMIT workmanship.
