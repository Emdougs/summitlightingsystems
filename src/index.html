FAQ

Common Questions

Does it look noticeable during the day?

No. The extruded aluminum track is designed to blend in and can be custom color-matched to your trim using RAL paint codes. Multiple track styles are available to suit different mounting scenarios.

How long do the lights last?

The LEDs are rated for 100,000 hours — that’s roughly 45 years at 6 hours of daily use. The system is backed by a 10-year comprehensive warranty.

Can they handle extreme weather?

Yes. The system carries an IP68 waterproof rating and is UL/cUL certified. Extension cables are UV-resistant and rated for 20+ years of outdoor exposure.

Is it safe?

Absolutely. It runs on a 48V low-voltage system — far safer than line voltage. Built-in reverse polarity protection means even a wiring mistake won’t damage the system.

Can I use warm white without the colors?

Yes. Each light has three dedicated white-spectrum diodes separate from the RGB diodes, so you get true, high-quality warm white — not a simulated mix.

How far can the lights run without extra equipment?

Up to 300 feet in a single uninterrupted run with no power injection needed, thanks to the 48V architecture and heavier 16-gauge wiring.

How are the lights controlled?

Through a smartphone app via Wi-Fi or Bluetooth. Every light is individually addressable, so you can create custom effects, schedule scenes, and control specific zones independently.

What’s included in the quote?

A custom-measured layout for your home, total cost, estimated timeline, and full warranty details — no surprises.

How does the $200 referral bonus work?

If you refer a neighbour, friend, or family member who books an install of $2,000 or more, we’ll send you $200 once the install is complete. There’s no cap — refer as many people as you like. Just have them mention your name in their quote request, or fill in the “Who referred you?” field on the form.
